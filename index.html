Our Track Record

Musiqaa has been around since yesterday. "Musiqaa" means music in Arabic. We made this choice because music is an anciet art, just like language. But unlike language, music can be understood by anyone who takes time to internalize the lovely echoes of sound. We realised that music holds immense power; for that reason we focus on bettering your auditory experience through our quality sound that relays the passion of the artists. Musiqaa helps you to fully immerse yourself in the beautiful art that is created by some wonderful artists -each with their own unique story. The different genres that we offer show the uniqueness that each of us holds within by matching our tastes. At Musiqaa we believe in music as sounds of the soul, not just sound. We hope that you heartily enjoy all that we have to offer.
